To add new SFXs, click the "Upload" () button.

To delete SFXs, right click on the SFX and then click "Delete Audio".

Sets are groups of up to 6 SFX.

To create a set, click the "New Set" () button.

To edit a set, click on the set name; it will appear in the "Set Editor".

To delete a set, right click on the set and then click "Delete Set".

Starred sets () are part of your "Favorites".

Add up to 5 sets to your "Favorites" list by dragging them from "Saved Sets" into this list.

Each set in your "Favorites" list corresponds to a button on your SFXer. To activate a set on your SFXer, press and hold button 6, then press on another button corresponding to the set you want to switch to.

Rearrange your favorites by dragging them to their desired positions. Remove sets within your "Favorites" list by right clicking on the set you wish to remove then clicking "Remove from Favorites".

Assign an SFX to a button by dragging it from your "Library" and dropping it into the desired button.

To rearrange SFX, drag them to the desired position.

To play an SFX, click the button.

To stop playing an SFX, click and hold the button for 1.5 seconds.

To remove an SFX, right click on the button and click "Empty Button".
